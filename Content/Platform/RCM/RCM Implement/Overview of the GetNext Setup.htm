Overview of the GetNext Setup

Using this feature, you can go over the list of available items and select a specific work item or control access to a pool of work items you do not want a user to view. It provides a quicker method for assigning a work item via a predefined logic and filtering criteria.

The Get Next feature is implemented as links in the toolbar as displayed in the following:

When an RCM application user clicks on the Get Next link, one of the following results occurs:

  • Depending on the type of assignment chosen, one of the following success messages appear when one or more work items are assigned to the user:
  • A new group of work items have been assigned to your business unit.
  • A new group of work item has been assigned to you and your business unit.
  • Too many relevant items found. [20] of them were assigned.
  • A new group of work items have been assigned to you.
  • A new work item has been assigned to your business unit.
  • A new work item has been assigned to you and your business unit.
  • A new work item has been assigned to you.
  • An error message appears indicating that there are already too many active work items.
