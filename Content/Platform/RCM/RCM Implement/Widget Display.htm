Widget Display

The recursive widget has all the capabilities of a regular records widget including filter, actions, records selection, paging and secondary layouts. For information on these capabilities, see The Records Widget.

The newly created widget will take space in the session’s widgets map. The internal widget will only take space in the map once the secondary layout is rendered.
