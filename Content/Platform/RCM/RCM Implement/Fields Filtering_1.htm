Fields Filtering

This filtering mechanism filters a specific field of the CWI.

To add a filtering filter, click the Add a Field to Filter button. A CWI field appears. If this is not the required field, click the arrow next to this field to open the drop-down list and select the required field.

In the filter column, define a filter for the specific field. The filter changes according to the data type of the field. Populate the filtering template for the field and click Accept.
