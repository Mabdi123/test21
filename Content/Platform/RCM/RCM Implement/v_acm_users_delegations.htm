v_acm_users_delegations

Description: List of delegations in RCM.

Field Name

Description

Delegation_join_id

The id of the delegation

From_user_join_id

ID of the ‘from’ user.

To_user_join_id

ID of the ‘to’ user.

From_date

Initial date of delegation

To_date

End date of delegation

Delegation_type

Either “Managed” or “Personal”

Fl_deleted

Indicates whether or not the delegation has been deleted

Fl_active

Indicates whether or not the delegation is active
