updateHierarchy

ACMResult updateHierarchy(in BusinessHierarchy businessHierarchy,
in BusinessUnit[] newBusinessUnitList,
in BusinessUnit[] updatedBusinessUnitList,
IntListHolder newBusinessUnitIdList)

Description: This method accepts full data for a business hierarchy and updates it in the RCM repository.

IN

  • businessHierarchy – Description of the updated hierarchy. Note that references to new nodes should be by their unique negative ID.
  • newBusinessUnitList – New business units that will be part of this hierarchy.
  • updatedBusinessUnitList – Existing business units that will be part of this hierarchy.

OUT

  • newBusinessUnitIdList – IDs of all newly created business units ordered as the “newBusinessUnitList” parameter.
