Access the Policies Window and Creating / Editing a Policy

To access the Policies Window

  1. Click on the General tab of the Reports pages to bring this page forward.
  2. Click beside the Policy field.

    The Policies window opens, enabling you to select the appropriate policies for the set that you want to define, or to edit any selected policy.

To create a new policy set:

  • Click in the upper right corner of the window.

The fields and options within the General page clear, enabling you to begin the new policy.

To edit an existing policy:

  • Select the policy that you want to edit from the list at the top of the window.

The selections within the pages of the Policies window refresh to reflect the selected policy. You can change any setting by accessing the appropriate tab for the setting that you want to change.

To set a different policy as default:

  1. Select the policy that you want to use as the new default from the list at the top of the window. The selections within the pages of the Policies window refresh to reflect the selected policy.
  1. Click in the upper right corner of the window. This policy is automatically selected each time you begin a new Report Definition.

To delete an existing policy:

  1. Select the policy that you want to delete from the list at the top of the window. The selections within the pages of the Policies window refresh to reflect the selected policy.
  1. Click in the upper right corner of the window. If there are existing Report Definitions that are associated with the selected policy, a message displays informing you of this connection.
