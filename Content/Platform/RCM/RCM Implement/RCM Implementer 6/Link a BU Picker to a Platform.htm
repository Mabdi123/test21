Link a BU Picker to a Platform List Field

You can link a BU picker containing business unit hierarchies that you defined in the Business Units tab to a platform list field. For details on setting up the business units, see Setting up Business Unit Hierarchies Setting up Business Unit Hierarchies.

To link a BU picker to a platform list field:

  1. In the RCM Designer, click the Platform Lists bar and open the relevant Platform List.
  1. Open the Fields tab and go to the relevant platform list field you want to link to a BU picker.

  1. In the Value Type field select BU Picker from the drop-down list.

NOTE:

The Type for a BU Picker Value Type must be set to String.
