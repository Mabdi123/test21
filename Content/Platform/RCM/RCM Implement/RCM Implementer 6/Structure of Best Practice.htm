Structure of Best Practice

Each Best Practice consists of the following sections:

  • Name of Best Practice and its description.
  • Steps of Best Practice: Steps are displayed in the order in which they should be made by the implementer. Each step has a name, icon and a Go button that when pressed points to the location.
