Assign Business Units to a Hierarchy

You can define the business unit hierarchy as follows.

To add a new business unit to a hierarchy:

  1. From the relevant Business Units Hierarchy page, click the Business Units tab to bring this page forward.
  1. Within the Business Units page, click within the toolbar on the left side of the page. The screen on the right side changes to enable you to define the properties for this new business unit.
  1. Using the options within the Business Unit Properties pane, provide a name for this new unit.
  1. Provide a description, if required. You can access a larger text area for the description by clicking the Edit button beside the Description field box.
  1. If there are business unit attributes that are relevant for the business unit that you are currently creating, click the Attributes tab to bring this page forward, and enter the appropriate values for the attributes that pertain to this unit, as shown in the example below:

To add an existing business unit to a hierarchy:

  1. From the relevant Business Units Hierarchy page, click the Business Units tab to bring this page forward.
  1. Within the Business Units page, click within the toolbar on the left side of the page.

When you move the cursor over a Node within the tree, the cursor changes to indicate that you can drop the unit in the current location.
