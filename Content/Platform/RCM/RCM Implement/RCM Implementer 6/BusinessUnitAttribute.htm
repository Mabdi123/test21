BusinessUnitAttribute

BusinessUnitAttribute{

intid

Stringidentifier;

Stringname;

EnumdataType;

String description;

}

Description: This object contains information regarding a business unit attribute

Fields:

  • id – RCM's internal unique identifier of the business unit attribute (Required)
  • identifier – The unique user-friendly identifier of the business unit attribute. Defined by the customer. (Required)
  • name – The unique display name of the attribute. Defined by the customer. (Required)
  • dataType – Type of the attribute. May be one of "String", "Integer", "Boolean" or "Float". (Required)
  • description – Description of the attribute. (Not Required.)
