Overview of General Configuration

The General Configuration within the Designer includes:

  • Business Types – Categories that enable you to group Custom Fields in order to allow the RCM end users to search for an entire category or specific field(s).
  • Advanced Field Styles – Enables you to define the fields’ display styles for alert and case views. This is achieved by defining Cascading Style sheet Attributes and then associating them with a specific field view. See also Advanced Field Styles, page 1.
