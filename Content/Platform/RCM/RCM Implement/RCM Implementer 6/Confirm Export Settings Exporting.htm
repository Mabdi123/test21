Confirm Export Settings & Exporting the Objects

  1. A screen that displays all the objects that you have selected to export appears.

Verify that these are the objects that you want to export and click the Export button.

  1. The Summary screen displays all the objects that were exported.

You have the option to save a log file for the procedure by clicking the Save Log File… button, and to click the Refresh Design Entities Pane checkbox to refresh the objects panes that is on the left side of the screen.

  1. Click the Finish button to complete the export procedure.
