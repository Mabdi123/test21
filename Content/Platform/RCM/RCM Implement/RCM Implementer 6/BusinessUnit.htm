BusinessUnit

BusinessUnit {

int id;

String identifier

String name;

String description;

BusinessUnitAttribute [] attributes;

}

Description: This object contains information regarding a Business unit.

Fields:

  • id – RCM's internal unique identifier of a business unit.  (Required.)
  • identifier – A unique user-friendly identifier of a business unit. Defined by the customer.  (Required.)
  • name – A business unit's display name. Defined by the customer. (Required.)
  • description – A business unit's description. Defined by the customer. (Not Required.)
  • attributes – An array of business unit attributes. See the section on BusinessUnitAttribute below for attribute description. (Required.)
