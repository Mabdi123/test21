Access the Workflow Options

You can begin the workflow process in the Alerts bar.

To access the tools and options for the alert workflows:

  • Click the Alerts bar and then click the Step Workflows tab in the Entities pane.

The icons available within the Step Workflows tab are as follows:

Icon

Description

Displays two tabs on the right side of the screen that enable you to define a new alert workflow definition. The Step Workflow tabs are:

  • General – Enables you to add a name, description, and Identifier for the new definition.
  • Workflow – Enables you to design and define the flow of the steps.

Selecting an alert workflow within the list and clicking this icon opens the General page on the right side of the screen, enabling you to edit the details of the selected alert workflow.

Deletes a selected alert workflow.

Duplicates the selected alert workflow.
