Data Structure for BusinessHierarchyInfo

BusinessHierarchyInfo {

int id;

String identifier;

String name;

}

Description: This object contains information regarding a business hierarchy. This is a subset of the BusinessHierarchyObject.

Fields:

  • id – RCM's internal unique identifier  of the business hierarchy. (Required.)
  • identifier – Unique identifier of a business hierarchy that is defined in RCM. (Required.)
  • name – A business hierarchy display name. (Required.)
