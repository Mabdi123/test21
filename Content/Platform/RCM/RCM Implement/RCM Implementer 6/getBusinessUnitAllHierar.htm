getBusinessUnitAllHierarchies

ACMResult getBusinessUnitAllHierarchies (in int businessUnitId, out BusinessHierarchyInfo[] businessUnitHierarchyArr);

Description: This method finds all BusinessHierarchies, which keep this BusinessUnit object, and returns array of the BusinessHierarchyInfo objects corresponded by these hierarchies.

IN

  • businessUnitId – Business unit's ID.

OUT

  • businessUnitHierarchyArr – Array of the business unit hierarchy info.
