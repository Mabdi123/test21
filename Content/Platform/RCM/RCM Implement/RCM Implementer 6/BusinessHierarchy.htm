BusinessHierarchy

BusinessHierarchy{

int id;

String identifier;

String name;

String description;

boolean showInACM;

BusinessHierarchyNode rootNode;

}

Description: This object contains information regarding business hierarchy.

Fields:

  • id – RCM's internal unique identifier of the hierarchy (Required.)
  • identifier – A business hierarchy's unique identifier. Defined by the customer. (Required.)
  • name – A business hierarchy's unique display name. Defined by the customer. (Required.)
  • description – A business hierarchy's description. Defined by the customer. (Not required.)
  • showInRCM – Indicates whether the business hierarchy is shown in the RCM. Defined by the customer. (Required.)
  • rootNode – Contains information about root node. (Required.) See the section on BusinessHierarchyNode in this page for attribute description.
