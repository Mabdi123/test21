Platform Lists Defined in the AIS

You can create lists that are originally defined by the AIS user. The RCM Designer enables connection to AIS lists as follows.

  1. From the Platform Lists tab, click the icon. A window opens.
  1. Select Base it on an existing list in AIS: The list is based on an existing list in the AIS.

  1. Click the Refresh icon of the AIS connection and select the relevant AIS server. This should be the AIS server that includes the Platform List that you want to define.
  1. Click the Refresh icon of the AIS List and click on its arrow to view all its Platform Lists. Select the specific AIS Platform List that you want to format.
  2. Enter the RCM display name for the platform list and click Finish icon. The General tab of Platform Lists opens.
