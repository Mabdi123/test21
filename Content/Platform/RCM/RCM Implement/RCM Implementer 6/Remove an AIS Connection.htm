Remove an AIS Connection

To remove an AIS connection click on the AIS connection and then click the action link Delete AIS Connection. A message appears:

Click OK in order to remove the AIS Connection.

NOTES:

  • You cannot delete any AIS connection that is currently in use.
  • You cannot select to remove more than one AIS connection at a single operation
  • You cannot remove a Virtual connection before you remove all of its related Tenant connections
