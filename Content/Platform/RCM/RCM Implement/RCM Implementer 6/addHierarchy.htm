addHierarchy

ACMResult addHierarchy(in BusinessHierarchy businessHierarchy,
in BusinessUnit[] newBusinessUnitList,
in BusinessUnit[] updatedBusinessUnitList,
out int businessHierarchyId,
out int[] newBusinessUnitIdList)

Description: This method accepts full data for a business hierarchy and registers it in the RCM repository.

IN

  • businessHierarchy – Description of the new hierarchy. Note that references to new nodes should be by their unique negative ID.
  • newBusinessUnitList – New business units that will be part of this hierarchy.
  • updatedBusinessUnitList – Existing business units that will be part of this hierarchy.

OUT

  • businessHierarchyId – ID of the created hierarchy for use with other APIs.
  • newBusinessUnitIdList – IDs of all newly created business units ordered as the “newBusinessUnitList” parameter.

NOTE:

Internal IDs should not be stored as they are not portable between environments. Refer to RCM uses two types of fields when identifying objects:, page 1.
