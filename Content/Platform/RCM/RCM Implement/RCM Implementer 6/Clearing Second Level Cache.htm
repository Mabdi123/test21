Clearing Second Level Cache

It is possible to remove all objects’ queries and collections from the ‘Hibernate’ related second level cache of the RCM.

Go to the Log Viewer page of the RCM by entering LogViewer.jsp
(e.g., http://server1:8080/rcm/LogViewer.jsp)

Clicking the Clear Cache button removes all second level and query caches.

You may use this button before initializing a migration process.
