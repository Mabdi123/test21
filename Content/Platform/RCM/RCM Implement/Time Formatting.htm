Time Formatting

Format guidelines:

Format

Details

h

Accepts hour in 12 hour format, one or two digits

hh

Same, but accepts only 2 digits

a

Accepts "AM" or "PM" case insensitive

H

Accepts hour in 24 hour format, one or two digits

HH

Same, but accepts only 2 digits

m

Accepts minutes in one or two digits

mm

Same, but accepts only 2 digits
