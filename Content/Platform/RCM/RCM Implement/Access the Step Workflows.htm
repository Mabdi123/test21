Access the Step Workflows Options

To access the tools and options for the Step Workflows:

  • Click the Cases bar and then click on the Step Workflows tab in the Entities pane.

The icons available within the Entities pane for this task are as follows:

Icon

Description

Displays two tabs on the right side of the screen that enable you to define a new Case Step Workflow Definition. The Case Workflow tabs are:

General – Enables you to add a name, description, and identifier for the new definition.

Workflow – Enables you to design and define the flow of the steps.

Selecting a case workflow within the list and clicking this icon opens the General page on the right side of the screen, enabling you to edit the details of the selected case workflow.

Deletes a selected case workflow.

Duplicates the selected case workflow.

Refreshes the Case Workflows list.
