Define a New Hierarchy

You can define a new hierarchy either by assigning exiting business units, which were previously defined, or you can begin a new hierarchy before business units are actually defined. In the latter case, you create the new business units as you define the hierarchy.

To define a new hierarchy:

  1. From the General bar – Business Units Hierarchy tab, click the icon. The screen on the right side changes to include two tabs: General and Business Units tabs.
  1. Using the options within the General tab, provide a name for this hierarchy.
  1. Provide a description, if required. You can access a larger text area for the description by clicking the Edit button beside the Description field box.
  1. Click the Business Units tab to bring this page forward if you want to add Business units to this hierarchy at this stage. You can assign business units later, by creating the business units separately or by selecting the Hierarchy again and clicking to edit it.
