BusinessUnitInfo

BusinessUnitInfo {

int id;

Stringidentifier;

Stringname;

}

Description: This object contains information regarding a business unit. Subset of the BusinessUnit object.

Fields:

  • id – RCM 's internal unique identifier of the business unit (Required.)
  • identifier – A unique identifier of a business unit. Defined by the customer. (Required.) 
  • name – A unique display name of a business unit. Defined by the customer. (Required.)
