Attributes

It is possible to define Business Unit Attributes that can be used for identifying or accessing business units. For example, if your organization distinguishes between different types of branches (e.g., Head Office, Regional Branches, etc.) you may define an attribute that enables you to indicate the category of a business unit. In this way, you can assign this category as an attribute to business offices, enabling users to map report parameters to this attribute.

You could, similarly, define an Address attribute, or a Branch number attribute that could be assigned to a business unit, when relevant. This would enable you to specify the appropriate information each time you define a business unit. Accessing the Hierarchy and Business Unit Options

To access the tools and options for the Business Unit Hierarchies and Business Units:

  • Click on the General bar and then click on the Business Units tab in the Entities pane (on the left side of the RCM Designer Screen).

The icons available within the Entities pane for this task are as follows:

For Designing Hierarchies:

Icon

Description

Displays two tabs on the right side of the screen that enable you to define a new hierarchy. (See Define a New Hierarchy.)

Selecting a hierarchy within the list of Business Unit Hierarchies and clicking this icon opens the Hierarchy pages on the right side of the screen, enabling you to edit the details of the selected hierarchy.

Deletes a selected hierarchy. You must close any page associated with this hierarchy before you can delete it. Note that deleting a hierarchy does not delete the business units that were defined for this hierarchy.

Duplicates the selected Business Unit Hierarchy.

Sets the selected hierarchy as the default hierarchy.

Refreshes the Business Unit Hierarchy list.

Toggles the display of the identifier.

For Designing Business Units:

Icon

Description

Displays two tabs on the right side of the screen that enable you to define a new Business Unit. The Business Units Tabs are:

  • General – Enables you to enter a name and description for this Business Unit.
  • Attributes – Enables you to assign a value for any Business Unit Attribute that is currently defined.

Selecting a Business Unit within the list of Business Units and clicking this icon enables you to edit the details of the selected Business Unit.

Opens a selected Business Unit within a Hierarchy that contains it.

If more than one Hierarchy contains this Business Unit, a pop up window opens, enabling you to select the hierarchy that you require.

Deletes a selected Business Unit.

Creates a copy of the Business Unit that is currently selected within the list of Business Units. The copy is assigned the name Copy of <name of selected Business Unit>.

Refreshes the list of Business Units.

NOTE:

When you create a new Business Unit while defining a hierarchy, the Business Unit only appears within the list of Business Units after performing a Save operation.

Opens the Business Unit Attributes window that enables you to define a new attribute or edit any existing attributes. The Business Unit Attributes are defined globally and can be used by all Business Units. (For more information see Define Business Unit Attributes).
