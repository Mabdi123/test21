Global Deadline

Some Items are configured to have a global deadline.

Depending on your organization’s implementation, deadlines are calculated according to a specific time frame which may include configuration based on business days calendars. These calendars enable you to set specific days as non business days which are not calculated in workflow and step deadlines. For details on platform list calendars, see Lists Business Calendar Lists.

Before this step occurs you may receive reminders and/or email notifications about the global deadline.
