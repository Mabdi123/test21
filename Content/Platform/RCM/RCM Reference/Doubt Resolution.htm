Doubt Resolution

You can manually resolve the doubt on an incoming entity.

Performing doubt resolution for specific entities is dependent on your relevant permissions.

The work items views include the following buttons:

Field

Description

Merge

Click to merge the incoming entity with an existing one. The system selects the candidate with the best score for the merge.

Remove Doubt

Click to remove doubt from the incoming entity.

You can resolve an entity in doubt from the Work Items list or from the item details page, as follows.
