Overview of Blotters

Trade Blotters can be automatically generated, according to the business requirements of the organization.

Each Trade Blotter presents trades that are organized according to the needs of your organization.

The license for this module is purchased separately.

The Blotters module enables users to:

  • Review the list of all Trade Blotters that are relevant for their role.
  • Open and review each trade in the Trade Blotter individually in order to confirm the content.
  • Add notes to Blotters, as required.
  • When appropriate, sign off Blotters to confirm the information. You have the option to sign off more than one Blotter at a time.
  • When appropriate, assign Blotters to different users.
  • When appropriate, assign Blotters to a different business unit.
  • Perform an ad hoc cross blotter query.
  • Click the Workbench tab and then click Blotters in the drop-down menu.
