Activate a Draft Policy - The Policy Activation Tab

Once you have finished creating the Draft Policy, you can activate it.

Before activating a Draft policy, verify that at least one rule has been set to ‘to be activated’. Otherwise, an error message appears indicating that no rules have been set for activation.

To activate a Draft Policy:

  1. Click the icon on the bottom of the window.

    2. The Policy Activation tab displays the list of rules that are to be activated.

  1. After reviewing the rules on this tab, click the icon.

    2. The Policy Activation table includes the column Activation Diff that provides the following states for each rule:

    • New - a new rule for this policy
    • Changed – a rule that has been changed for this policy.
    • Unchanged – a rule that has not been changed for this policy.
    • Deactivated - a rule that has been deactivated for this policy.

    Note that this tab also has filtering mechanism. You can use this feature by clicking the Filter icon. For details, see Filter a Rule.

  1. The Policy Activation tab name switches to Pending Policy. Once the policy is activated the name of the tab switches back to Policy Activation.
  2. A message also appears indicating that a policy activation request is pending.

    Click the here link to view the content of the policy.
  3. The Actimize Intelligence Server (AIS) must now process the rules of the policy. The interval for this procedure depends on the organization’s configuration of the AIS. Note that during this interval you cannot modify the Draft Policy. If you would like to modify the Draft policy then you must cancel the activation request by clicking the icon that appears on the screen. To check that the policy is now active, click the Active Policy tab. It is recommended to refresh the data on this web page by pressing the F5 key.
  4. The rules of the Active policy appear on this window. If the rules do not appear on window then click on the Activation History icon to view the status of this activation. For details, see Historical Policies Tab - History of Activated Policies.
