Case Items Toolbar

The case items buttons that appear in the toolbar depend on the needs of your organization and the permissions that are assigned to your role. The following are typical actions that are available from the Case Items list window.

The following case items buttons appear on the toolbar.

  • The buttons that appear within the Case Items toolbar depend on the configuration required by your organization. Your organization may also customize the display of the buttons or add buttons for a customized task. This guide presents all available actions. If an action is not supported within your organization’s workflow, or if it is not included within your permitted views, it will not appear on the toolbar.
  • You must select a case item to enable some of the toolbar buttons.
  • Selecting one or more case items may cause toolbar buttons to be enabled or disabled depending on your organization’s configuration.
