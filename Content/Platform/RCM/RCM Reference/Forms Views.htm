Forms Views

The Forms views menu enables you to select the relevant view.

You can select the view of outgoing forms that is appropriate for you by clicking the arrow beside the current view within the title bar of the outgoing forms list.

A drop-down list opens, showing all views that are appropriate for your tasks.

The views that appear within the list depend on the requirements of each organization and the roles that you perform.

The following are examples of views and options that may be available.

Option

Description

All Forms

Shows all forms that the logged on user has permission to view.

My Forms

Shows only forms that are assigned to the user who is currently logged on to the RCM application.

My In Process Forms

Shows all forms of the logged on user that are in the In Process status.

Closed Forms

Includes all forms that their status indicates that they are closed (Reported Successfully, Closed as non issue, Reported externally).
