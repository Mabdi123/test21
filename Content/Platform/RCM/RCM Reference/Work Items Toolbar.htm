Work Items Toolbar

The Work Items buttons appear on the toolbar.

The buttons that appear within the Work Items toolbar depend on the configuration required by your organization. Your organization may also customize the display of the buttons or add buttons for a customized task. This guide presents all available actions.
If an action is not supported within your organization’s workflow, or if it is not included within your permitted views, it will not appear on the toolbar.
You must select a work item to enable some of the toolbar buttons. Selecting one or more work items may cause toolbar buttons to be enabled or disabled depending on your organization’s configuration.
