Select the Relevant Business Unit(s) for All Dashboard Reports


Click the icon beside the Business Unit field to open the Business Unit Explorer, which enables you to select or enter the required business unit.

Use the Business Unit Hierarchy drop-down menu to select the required hierarchy.

Once you select a hierarchy, the Hierarchy Tree shows the highest level of business units within this hierarchy.

Click in the tree to open a node and display the business units that are sub-units of the selected node.

Include Descendants: If you would like to include all the descendants of the business unit within the selected hierarchy, select Yes from the drop-down menu.
