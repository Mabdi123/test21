Activity Center

The license for this feature is purchased separately.

This feature enables you to receive notifications regarding actions on work items that are relevant for you. You can receive notice that a work item was assigned to you. You can also receive a notification for an RFI reply on an item that you are the owner of. Other notifications may be added depending on your organization's implementation.

A bell icon appears with a number indicating the amount of notifications you have. ( supports up to five messages).

When you click the bell icon, your notifications are displayed.

  • Click the notification to go to the relevant item or list of RFIs on an item.
  • Click the text link on the bottom to go to your pre-configured display location.
The bottom link may not appear depending on your organization's configuration.
