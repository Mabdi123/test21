Reset a Step

Resetting a Step enables you to go back to the initial step of the item.

Certain steps enable you to perform the Reset action. This depends on the permissions that your organization provided for you.

To reset a step:

  • Click the Change Step button and select the Reset action from the drop-down menu. The action indicates the initial step that the case will have after this action (e.g., Reset To: Ready). You may be required to add a note as part of this action.
  • The case is changed back to its initial step.

Activities that were done prior to the Reset action are kept in the history view. A Reset action can be performed despite unchecked mandatory actions. A Reset action can be made more than once.

When the Next Steps icon is grayed out it indicates that there are no more steps in the workflow. You may still move backwards in the workflow via the Change Step button according to your defined permissions.
