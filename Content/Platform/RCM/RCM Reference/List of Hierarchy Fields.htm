List of Hierarchy Fields

To understand Risk Based Reviewing, you should initially know the term list of hierarchy fields. This term is illustrated in the following example.

A Blotter Type has an associated trade type, which has the following custom fields:

  • Product Type (e.g. “Equity”, “Fixed Income” or “Futures”)
  • Trader (e.g. “John Doe”, “Silvia Rosner”, etc.)
  • Product (e.g. “MSFT”, “IBM”, “GOOG”, etc.)

A three level hierarchy is then defined and is composed of the following levels:

  1. Product Type
  1. Trader
  1. Product
