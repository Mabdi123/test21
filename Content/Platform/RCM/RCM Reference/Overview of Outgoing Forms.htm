Overview of Outgoing Forms

The Outgoing Forms module provides a list of all the forms that have been added to cases. It enables you to:

  • Review the available forms including their dates, status and owners
  • Quickly filter and sort the outgoing forms in order to find specific forms
  • Easily identify forms’ amendments
  • Jump to the form’s related cases.
  • Print, export and show a form as a PDF

Outgoing forms are usually regulatory forms that can be filled in and later filed to a regulatory agency. Your organization may also provide you with customized forms that may be used as questionnaires and other user input.
