Overview Window

The Overview Window has a rectangular marquee that represents the Drawing Canvas viewport. The marquee is movable.

It can be used for the following:

  • Pan the drawing to a specific direction on the canvas: Click and drag the mouse while it is inside the rectangular marquee of the Overview window. The cursor changes its shape to that of a closed hand () indicating that the rectangular marquee can be moved. The drawing area that is inside the rectangular marquee is displayed on the canvas.
  • Zoom and pan a specific area of the drawing: Move the mouse to one of the links of the rectangular marquee that is on the Overview Window. The cursor changes its shape to a two headed arrow (). Click and drag the mouse in order to change the size of the rectangular marquee. You can now pan the square to a specific part of the drawing. The drawing area that is inside the rectangular marquee is displayed on the canvas.
