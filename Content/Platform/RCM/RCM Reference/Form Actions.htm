Form Actions

The Outgoing Forms actions appear in the toolbar.

  • The actions that appear within the Outgoing Forms toolbar depend on the configuration required by your organization. This guide presents all available actions. If an action is not supported within your organization’s workflow, or if it is not included within your permitted views, it does not appear within the toolbar.
  • Selecting one or more forms may cause toolbar buttons to be enabled or disabled depending on your organization’s configuration
