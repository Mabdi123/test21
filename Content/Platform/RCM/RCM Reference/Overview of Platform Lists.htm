Overview of Platform Lists

Platform Lists enable you to add or remove items to the list and optionally approve or disapprove individual items. Note that only users with required permissions are able to approve or disapprove an item of a list.

Click the Settings tab and click Platform Lists in the drop-down menu to open the Platform Lists module.

The displayed options and parameters are based on your specific solution, and therefore may appear entirely different from the examples in this guide. For solution specific information, refer to your solution documentation.

Additional key features include the following:

  • Two lists for business calendars. For details see, Business Calendar Lists.

Do not delete the Business Calendars – Days, or Business Calendars – BU Associations lists from the .

  • Viewing the history of actions for a specific list.
  • Editing the values of an entry.
  • Adding/removing entries to a specific list.
  • Exporting/importing the items of a list.

The Platform List can function with up-to approximately 100,000 records (items) in a list.
