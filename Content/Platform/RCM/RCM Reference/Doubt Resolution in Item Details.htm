Doubt Resolution in Item Details

You can review an entity in doubt’s details and view the possible match candidates before performing doubt resolution.

To perform doubt resolution:

  1. Click number of entities link for an item in doubt

    or

    Click the Item ID of the entity in doubt and then the Doubt tab in the Item Details page.

  1. The Doubt page opens, displaying the entity and all of the potential merge candidates.

  1. Click to navigate to an existing item’s details.

For a merge:

  1. The default candidate with the highest score is selected. You can select a different entity candidate. You can also select specific attribute fields that you want the merged entity to retain.

    2. Some fields cannot be selected in the merge process. This may be because they are:

    • Fields with the identical results to the incoming entity.
    • Fields that have been disabled for the user in implementation.
  1. Click the Merge button. The following message is displayed

  1. Click Yes and the entity is merged.

For removing doubt:

  1. Click the Remove Doubt button for the item in doubt.

    2. The following message is displayed:

  1. Click Yes to remove doubt. The item is then not listed as in doubt.
