Cross out a Constraint or a Condition

When a condition/constraint is crossed out, it is no longer part of the sequence. However, it can be easily returned to the sequence by removing the cross out line from it.

To cross out a condition/constraint:

  1. Hover over the condition/constraint that you want to remove and then clear the checkbox next to it.
  1. To return the condition/constraint to the sequence, hover over the crossed out condition/constraint and then select the checkbox next to it.
