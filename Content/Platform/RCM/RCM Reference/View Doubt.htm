View Doubt

The doubt functionality enables you to determine which entities are merged. An entity in doubt is a person, counter-party, institution or other entity that entered the RCM and is potentially matched to more than one entity in the system or has a questionable match to an existing entity. Work items and their doubt status may be displayed in the Work Items list view, as follows.

When entity matching is enabled the following columns may be displayed in the work items view:

Field

Description

In Doubt

Yes: The item is in doubt and you can perform doubt resolution.

No: The item is not in doubt, meaning it is not potentially matching other items in the system.

Existing Entities

Displays the number of existing items that are candidates for merge. Click on the number of entities link to open the Doubt tab for the specific entity.
