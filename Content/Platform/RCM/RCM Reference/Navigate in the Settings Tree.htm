Navigate in the Settings Tree

The following is an example of the Settings Tree.

  • To view the entire tree with all its branches click the Expand All link.
  • To collapse the tree, click the Collapse All link.
  • Click to expand a folder in the tree.
  • Click to collapse a folder in the tree.
  • Click a leaf to view the specific setting or list item.
  • To move to the next leaf in the tree, click the Next link. The selected leaf’s data is then loaded on the right pane of the screen.
  • To move to the previous leaf in the tree, click the Previous link. The selected leaf’s data is then loaded on the right pane of the screen.
