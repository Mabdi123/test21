Blotter Trades

You can view all the Trades of any Trade Blotter by clicking the Blotter Identifier link that is associated with each Trade Blotter within the main list of Trade Blotters. There are regular Blotter trades as well as risk based trades. Click the relevant link to display the trade information.

The license for this module is purchased separately.

When Trades Blotters are available, one of the fields that you see within the list is a link to the Trades List. However, since each organization has different requirements for the Trade Blotter List content, a different field may be used as this link.

The Trades List of the selected Trade Blotter replaces the Trade Blotter list on the screen.

The available options and the Trades List that are presented within this window depend on the Trade Blotter Type and on your organization’s requirements.

When you click a Blotter link, the Trade details are displayed.
