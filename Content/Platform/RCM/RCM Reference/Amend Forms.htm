Amend Forms

If a form was completed and has the status of Reported – pending or Reported – success, it cannot be edited. If information in the form needs to be changed, the form can be opened and amended. This creates a duplicate of the form (with a new name and ID).

After the duplicated form is edited, the form is returned back to the Ready for report status so that it can be used to generate a new report (via the Finish button). Reports are generated from both the original and duplicated forms.

To amend a form:

  1. In RCM, select the Cases tab, and click the relevant Case ID link.

  1. In the Case Details window, click the Forms tab.

  1. Open the relevant form by clicking the link in the Identifier column. The selected form opens.

  1. Click Amend.

    2. A duplicate form is created with a new name and ID, as shown below. A full copy of all attachments is made as well. The new name assigned to the amended form is <original name – Amendment>.

    The form status changes to In process.

  1. Complete the duplicated form as required and click either:
    • Save to save the form and complete it later, or
    • Finish to submit the report and change its status to Ready for report.
