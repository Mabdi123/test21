Case Views

You can select the view of cases that is appropriate for you by clicking the arrow beside the current view within the title bar of the Case List. .

A drop-down list opens, showing all views that are appropriate for your tasks.

The views that appear within the list depend on the requirements of each organization and the roles that you perform.

The following are examples of views and options that may be available.

Option

Description

All Cases

Shows all open cases that the logged on user has permission to view.

My Cases

Shows only open cases that are assigned to the user who is currently logged on to the Risk Case Manager application.

Closed Cases

Shows cases that have been closed.

Aging 30 days

Shows open cases that are 30 days old.
