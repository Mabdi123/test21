Spell Checker

Spellchecking is available for all text boxes in the . Text boxes include notes, description fields and various form fields.

Spellcheck when the Rich Text Feature is Enabled

Click the Spellchecking button to start the spellchecking procedure.

All misspelled words and the Spellchecking is done button are highlighted.

Correct the spelling errors manually or click on the word in order to view a list of suggested words. Click on the suggested word in order to replace it with displayed word. Clicking Ignore indicates that the word should not be regarded as misspelled.

In some organizations there may be more than one spellchecking dictionary. You have the option to click and select the default spellchecking language.

Spellcheck when the Rich Text Feature is Disabled

Click the Spellchecking link to start the spellchecking procedure.

All misspelled words are highlighted.

Correct the spelling errors manually or click on the word in order to view a list of suggested words. Click on the suggested word in order to replace it with displayed word. Clicking Ignore indicates that the word should not be regarded as misspelled.

To recheck a text area for spelling errors, click the Recheck link. In some organizations there may be more than one spellchecking dictionary. You have the option to click on the arrow of the displayed language link (e.g., English) and select the default spellchecking language.
