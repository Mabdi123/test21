Business Calendar Lists

The business calendar platform lists are lists that enable you to configure calendars with dates that are designated as non-business days and associate the calendars with BUs. This is relevant for work item workflow and email deadlines.

Click the calendar link and click the New Item button to add a new item to the list.

The Business Calendars – BU Associations list contains the following fields.

Option

Description

Business Unit

Select the BU you want to associate with this calendar name.

Click the icon beside the Business Unit field to open the Business Unit Explorer, which enables you to select or type the business unit that you are searching for.

To select a business unit:

  1. Use the Business Unit Hierarchy drop-down list to select the hierarchy that you currently require.
  1. Once you select a hierarchy, the Hierarchy Tree shows the highest level of business units within this hierarchy.
  1. Click in the tree to open a node and display the business units that are subordinates of the selected node.
  1. Click the Business Unit name to select it and click OK to select it for assignment. Once selected, this business unit appears within the Assign to Business Unit / User window.

Calendar Name

Enter the calendar name you want to associate with the relevant BU.
