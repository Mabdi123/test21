Trade Actions

The Buttons that appear within the Trades toolbar depend on the configuration required by your organization. This guide presents all available actions. If an action is not supported within your organization’s workflow, or if it is not included within your permitted views, it will not appear within the toolbar.

The following actions may be available within the toolbar:

Review

Un-Review

Add Note

View Notes

Print Menu

Create a New Work Item

View History

Sign-Off
