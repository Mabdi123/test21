Overview of Lists

Each list, defined within this module, displays all items (e.g., accounts or any other type of entity required for your organization) that should be excluded/included during generation of work items. The list names appear within the Lists activity bar, usually under the Work Item Type that they refer to, however display conventions for the Lists module depends on the configuration required by your organization.

Click the Settings tab and click Lists in the drop-down menu to open the Lists page.

The displayed options and parameters are based on your specific solution, and therefore may appear entirely different from the examples in this guide. For solution specific information, refer to your solution documentation.

Click to display the Activity bar.

When you select a link under the Lists Activity bar, a list of all threshold definitions, which are currently defined for the selected list, appears within the window.

The columns within this window are defined according to the specific requirements of the selected list. This window enables you to select a row within the list by clicking on the selection option in order to edit this row.
