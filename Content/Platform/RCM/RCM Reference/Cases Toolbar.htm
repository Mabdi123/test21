Cases Toolbar

This topic describes the main actions and options available from the cases toolbar.

  • The actions that appear within the Cases toolbar depend on the configuration required by your organization. This guide presents all available actions. If an action is not supported within your organization’s workflow, or if it is not included within your permitted views, it will not appear in the toolbar.
  • Selecting one or more cases may cause toolbar buttons to be enabled or disabled depending on your organization’s configuration.

  • Depending on the configuration of your RCM, some actions may only appear in the toolbar when you access the case details.
