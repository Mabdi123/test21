Move to the Next Case Item

In order to quickly navigate through a list of case items you can have the system automatically display the Case Item details of the next case item after the step has been changed.

To open the Change Step window with this option:

  1. Click the Item ID of a case item to open the Case Item Details page.
  1. Click the Change Step button in the Case Item Details page to open the Change Step window.

  1. Select the Move to the next work item in the list after changing this step checkbox.
  1. After the step is changed the system automatically displays the item details of the next item in the relevant view.
  • The next item is selected according to the current view.
  • For the last item on the list, it remains in the current Item Details page.
  • The checkbox is only displayed when you access the Change Step window from the Item Details page.
  • The checkbox selection is retained for the next time the Change Step window is accessed from item details, according to your relevant personalization setup.
