View Previous Steps

The buttons above the tasks table display the current Step of the case and each of its previous steps in chronological order. The button for the current step of the work item appears in light green with darker green text. Buttons for previous steps are off-white with gray text, indicating that they are for viewing only.

To view the tasks of previous Steps

  • From the Case à Workflow screen, click a specific previous step (e.g., Escalated).

The tasks table of the step appears.

A tasks table of a previous step can only be viewed, and therefore, no changes can be made to it.
