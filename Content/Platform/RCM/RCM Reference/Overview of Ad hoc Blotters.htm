Overview of Ad-hoc Blotters

The trading environments of institutional broker/dealers require the ability to create queries that span on multiple desks and multiple trading days in order to comply with the compliance demands. This type of investigation enables you to perform an ad-hoc review of the overall trading activity. It also provides you with the option to save the results of the investigation as a work item. Results of the investigation also display the specific branch managers’ work items and notes on trades

In the Research menu, click the Ad-hoc Blotters link or in the Blotters page click the Ad-hoc Blotters button. The Ad-hoc blotters page opens.

Note that the initial view does not display any trades.
