Export/Mail/Print

The Export/Mail/Print Actions appear in the Export menu in the Case Items toolbar.

These actions may appear in the toolbar. Buttons and action links are customizable depending on your organization implementation.

The Export links allow you to:

  • Print a work items list
  • Print the work items details
  • Export information about work items to external files
  • Send work items details via email,
  • Convert selected work items to PDF files.
