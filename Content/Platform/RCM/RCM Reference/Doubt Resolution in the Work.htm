Doubt Resolution in the Work Items List

You can resolve an entity in doubt through the Work Items list.

To perform a merge:

  1. Select an item that is in doubt.
  2. In the More menu in the toolbar, click the Merge button to merge this entity with an existing one.

    The following message is displayed:

  1. Click Yes to merge the entity. The item is then merged with an existing entity.

The item with the earliest date of the merged items is displayed in the work items list.

To remove doubt from an entity:

  1. Select an item that is in doubt.
  1. Click the Remove Doubt button for the selected item.

    The following message is displayed:

  1. Click Yes to remove doubt. The item is then not listed as an entity in doubt.
