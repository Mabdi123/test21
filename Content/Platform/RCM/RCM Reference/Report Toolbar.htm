Report Toolbar

The following describes the main actions and options available from the Reports toolbar.

  • The actions that appear within the Reports toolbar depend on the configuration required by your organization. This guide presents all available actions. If an action is not supported within your organization’s workflow, or if it is not included within your permitted views, it will not appear in the toolbar.
  • Selecting one or more reports may cause toolbar buttons to be enabled or disabled depending on your organization’s configuration.
