Drill Down to a Specific Hierarchy

  • Click a link of a specific group in the Trades Summary table to be moved to the Trade Details view of these trades.

The specific filtered hierarchy is displayed on the yellow heading.

  • Click Clear to remove the hierarchy.

  • You can add an additional manual filter to the filtered hierarchy by using the filter.

For additional information on using the filter, see Filter Options for Risk Based Trades. You have the option to review and sign off the specific trades. For additional information on reviewing a trade, see Review.

To return to the Trade Summary view, click the Trades Summary link. The view does not display the updated status of all trades automatically.
