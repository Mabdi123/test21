Recall a Step

Recalling a step enables you to go back to the previous step as part of the workflow transition.

Certain steps enable a Recall action. This depends on the permissions that your organization provided for you.

To recall a step:

  1. Click the Change Step button.

    2. The Change Step window opens.

  1. From the Change Step window, select the Recall action from the drop-down menu. The action indicates the step that the case item will have after this action (e.g., Recall To: In Process). You may be required to add a note as part of this action. The Item is changed back to its previous step.

Activities that are done prior to the Recall action are kept in the history view. A Recall action can be performed despite unchecked mandatory actions. A Recall action can be performed more than once.

When the Next Steps icon is grayed out it indicates that there are no more steps in the workflow.

You may still move backwards in the workflow via the Change Step button according to your defined permissions.
