Blotters Workflow

Your organization may enable the Blotters to support more than the standard two steps (open, signoff). The following steps can be supported:

  • Ready: The Blotter initial step (after it was distributed and ready for work)
  • In Process: Automatic step once you start working on the Blotter.
  • Under Review: You can move manually to this step.
  • Signed off: Automatic step you signed off the Blotter.

You cannot change the Blotter workflow step to Signed Off manually. Only a Blotter actual sign off operation triggers this change.
