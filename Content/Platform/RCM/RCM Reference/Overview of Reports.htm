Overview of Reports

Reports can be automatically generated according to the business requirements of the organization, or they can be generated by the user (on demand) according to that user’s specific needs, as follows:

  • Management Reports – providing statistical analysis about work items, workloads and other activity.
  • Supervision Reports – providing Supervisors with the tools to monitor and sign off reports that are under their supervision.

See also On-Demand Reports.

The Reports module enables you to:

  • Generate relevant reports either as DHTML for immediate purposes or as a static file for saving
  • Review, filter and sort the list of all reports that are available for their tasks
  • Open and review each report individually
  • View a summary of report information
  • Add notes to reports, as required
  • When appropriate, sign off reports to confirm the information
  • When appropriate, assign a report to a different user
  • When appropriate, assign reports to a different business unit

Click the Workbench tab and then click Reports in the drop-down menu.
