Overview of the Application

 

The Work Manager (NWM) application enables you to capture, distribute and automate data within your organizational systems. You can gather items that relate to various workflow processes and prioritize and route the work to the correct resources.

The Work Manager tools ensure that:

  • Authorized staff receives scored and filtered work items, each accompanied with all related work item information.
  • Work items are routed and handled according to the firm’s procedures, tracked and audited through resolution, providing evidence of principal review.
  • Internal reporting is available as a built-in feature, together with options for sending work item information by email and exporting information to other file formats.

The Work Manager is built in a modular fashion, enabling each organization to select those modules that fit their needs.

Each module can also be presented within the application according to specific users’ needs, enabling management and System Administrators to access modules that are appropriate for their roles, without displaying these modules for all users.
