Activity Bars

Depending on your organizational requirements, an activity bar may be available instead of and/or in addition to the toolbar.

The activity bar actions correspond to the relevant toolbar button.

Depending on your organization’s implementation, the activity bars can be made visible.

Following is an example of an activity bar, when enabled:

  • Click beside the bar name (or click on the bar name) to open it and view the associated activities. Click to close the bar.
  • Click in the middle of any activity box to minimize the box and places a maximize icon beside the list area.
  • You can re-open the bars by clicking the maximize icon.
