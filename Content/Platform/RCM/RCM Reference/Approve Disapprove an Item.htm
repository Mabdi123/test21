Approve/Disapprove an Item

The Four Eye Review approval feature is configured in implementation if you want to enforce an approval for every new record that is added to the list by the user. It requires an additional user with the relevant permissions to approve new and edited items. All items must be approved by at least two people.

Click on a specific Platform List name to view its contents and additional actions.

  • To approve an item select it and click the Approve button. An approved item can no longer be edited. Approved items are displayed with the icon next to them.
  • To disapprove an item select it and click the Disapprove button.

Only users with the required permissions may approve or disapprove an item. There may be Platform Lists that were configured not to have the Approve or Disapprove options.
