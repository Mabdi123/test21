Single Case Item Selection

Select a case item and click Change Step in the toolbar. The Change Step window opens displaying the Reasons tab.

When you select the Next Step for the case item the Reasons tab is displayed. The tab lists the available reasons for the selection of the next step in alphabetical order. You can also de-select a reason to remove the reason from the case item reasons.

If it is mandatory to select a reason when changing the step, an asterisk is displayed on the Reasons tab, meaning you cannot change the step without selecting a reason.

The selected reasons at the top of the list are those that were previously added for steps of the relevant case item.

The clock icon indicates which step the reason was selected for.

If there are no reasons for the selected case item then the Reasons tab is not displayed.

  • If the Reasons tab is displayed but the list is either empty or partially displayed, it may be because of your organization’s configurations.
  • For Reset and Recall actions there are only previously selected reasons displayed. These actions do not require you to select reasons. You can de-select reasons related to the step that you returned to during reset and recall.
  • The selected reasons can be viewed in the case item history.
