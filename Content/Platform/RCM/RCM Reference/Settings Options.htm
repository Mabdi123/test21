Settings Options

The options that appear in the upper center and right corner of the window depend on the permissions assigned to the currently logged on user and on the requirements of each type of setting. For example, some settings may support the addition of a new item to the setting, other settings may only support editing information within a selected row, while other settings may not support addition or editing.

You can select (click the radio button beside) a row within the setting in order to perform any of the standard operations that appear in the upper right corner of the window. These standard options are described in the following.
