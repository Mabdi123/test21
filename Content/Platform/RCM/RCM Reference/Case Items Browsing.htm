Case Items Browsing

Whenever a Case Item List goes beyond the size of the configured row limit the list is presented in multiple pages, which groups the items (For example: case items 1 – 20, 21- 30, etc.).

You can view the items in groups of 20 or in groups of 100 items. When the case items are presented in groups of 20, a link appears on the far right of the browsing options that lets you toggle the view to groups of 100. Once in this view, the link changes to . If the full list is less than 100, the link toggles between Show as Pages and Show all.

Use the next and previous page options to jump to the next or previous group of items or to jump directly to the first page or the last page.
