Case Browsing Options

Whenever a Case List goes beyond the size of the configured row limit the list is presented in multiple pages that group the cases (For example: cases 1 – 20, 21- 30).

You can view the cases in groups of 20 or in groups of 100. When the cases are presented in groups of 20, a link appears on the far right of the browsing options that lets you toggle the view to groups of 100. Once in this view, the link changes to . (If the full list is less than 100, the link toggles between Show 20 and Show all.)

Use the next and previous page options to jump to the next or previous group of items or to jump directly to the first page or the last page.
