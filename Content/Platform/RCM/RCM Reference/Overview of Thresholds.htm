Overview of Thresholds

The list of thresholds is configured according to your organization’s needs. Each detection model may include thresholds which are analyzed by an AIS Model according to the conditions set for that model.

Click the Settings tab and click Thresholds in the drop-down menu to open the Thresholds module.

The displayed threshold options and parameters are based on your specific solution, and therefore may appear entirely different from the examples in this guide. For solution specific information, refer to your solution documentation.

Click to display the Activity bar.

Each link in the Thresholds Activity menu opens a list of relevant thresholds.

The columns that display within this table depend on the specific requirements of the selected setting. Similarly, the options that are relevant for each setting vary, according to the specific requirements of that setting.
