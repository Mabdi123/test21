The Trade Views

Click a specific Trade to progress to one of the following views:

Trade Summary view

This is the default view of Trade Blotter Types that have Trade Summary definitions. The RCM implementer of your organization decides which blotter types have Trade Summary definitions. The Trade Details view displayed the Risk Based Trade details in the same way regular trades are displayed.

The Trade Summary view provides advanced criteria for signing off a trade. An average score of a pre defined list of hierarchy fields (defined by implementer on the blotter type) is compared to a threshold that is predefined by the Implementer. All trades that exceed this threshold must be reviewed before the blotter can be signed off.

The Trade Summary includes:

Thresholds: Indicates the threshold according to aggregation in a specific column.

Range: Indicates the threshold range for the relevant risk based trades.

Totals: Indicates the total number of trades, how many are reviewed, and how many must be reviewed but have not yet been.

The following sign-off criteria is deployed:

  • The list of hierarchy fields and individual trades that have a score greater or equal to the displayed high risk must be reviewed. When the icon is displayed for a specific list of hierarchy fields then its trades must be reviewed. The icon that is displayed for a specific list of hierarchy fields indicates that its trades have been reviewed.
  • You can select a trade and click the Review button to review the trade. The trade is automatically updated and a green check is then displayed beside the trade. When you click Trade Summary the view opens but the data is not automatically updated. Click to recalculate the trade information.

If you attempt to sign off a Blotter with high score trades that have not been reviewed then a message will appear and indicate that you cannot sign off this Blotter

  • All lists of hierarchy fields and individual trades that have a score between the displayed high risk and the displayed medium risk are recommended to be reviewed but it is not mandatory.

To switch from the Trades Summary view to the Trades Details view, click the Trades Details link.

Trade Details view

This is the default view of Trade Blotter Types that do not have Trade Summary definitions. This view is identical to the trade details of the regular trades.
