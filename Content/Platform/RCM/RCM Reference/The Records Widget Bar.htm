The Records Widget Bar

The Records widget bar is available out of the box as follows.

According to your organization's settings the Records Widget bar may include some or all of the following options:

  • Predefined actions may have been defined by your organization for the results of the query. To use a specific action, select it from the drop-down menu and click the Execute button.
  • The Reset button removes all the filtering and sorting definitions that you have added.
  • The Refresh button updates the results data on display, without changing any filter selection that may be in effect.
  • The Count Results link provides information on the exact number of records in the Results table.
  • The Export button enables you to export the records information.
