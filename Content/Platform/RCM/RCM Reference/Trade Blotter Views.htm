Trade Blotter Views

Select the view of Trade Blotters that is appropriate for you by clicking the arrow beside the current view within the title bar of the Trade Blotter List. A drop-down list opens, showing all views that are appropriate for your tasks.

The Views that appear within the list depend on the requirements of each organization and the roles that you perform.

The following are examples of views and options that may be available.

Option

Description

Open Trade Blotters

Shows all Blotters that have not been signed off.

All Trade Blotters

Shows all Trade Blotters that the logged on user has permission to view.

Signed Trade Blotters

Shows only Trade Blotters that have been signed off.

Signed and/or Open Trade Blotters

Shows only those Trade Blotters that have not yet been materialized.

Signed and materialized Trade Blotters

Shows Blotters that have been materialized.
