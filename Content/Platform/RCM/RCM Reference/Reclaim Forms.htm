Reclaim Forms

If a completed form was not yet generated as a report, and has one of the following form statuses, it cannot be edited:

  • Ready for report
  • Reported – Rejected
  • Closed – Non issue
  • Closed – Reported externally

If information in a form with one of the above statuses needs to be edited, users can reclaim the form.

After the reclaimed form is edited, the form is returned back to the Ready for report status so that it can be used to generate a report (via the Finish button). A report is generated only from the updated form.

To reclaim a form:

  1. In RCM, select the Cases tab, and click the relevant Case ID link.

  1. In the Case Details window, click the Forms tab.

  1. Open the relevant form by clicking the link in the Identifier column. The selected form opens.

  1. Click Reclaim.

    2. The form status changes to In process.

  1. Edit the reclaimed form as required and click either:
    • Save to save the form and complete it later, or
    • Finish to submit the report and change its status to Ready for report.
