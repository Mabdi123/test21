Actions for the Tenant

The tenant has the capability to create and manage private rules. These are rules that are created and used by the tenant only on the tenant's transactions and are not available to any other tenant (excluding the host).

Activate Common Rules

The tenant has the capability to decide whether or not to activate Common Rules that were defined by the host. The tenant can decide not to change the host’s recommendations for rules and in such scenarios the rules will be activated on the tenant’s transactions according to the host’s original recommendation.

Available Actions for Tenant

The tenant can perform the following actions:

  • Create/ Modify/remove new private rules
  • Activate/deactivate private rules.
  • Activate/deactivate common rules, including changing the Host’s original activation recommendation.
  • View history of the rule but not the history of the entire policy

The tenant does not have the following capabilities that are available for the host:

  • Define priority rules
  • Define Test Rules
  • Export/import rules
