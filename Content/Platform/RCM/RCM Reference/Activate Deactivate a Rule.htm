Activate/Deactivate a Rule

Activating a rule indicates that the rule will be activated during the next time the Activate Policy icon is clicked.

To activate a rule:

  1. From the More Actions drop-down list, select Activate.

    2. The Planned State of the rules changes to Active in the Draft Policy tab:

  1. Click the green Activate… button at the bottom of the Draft Policy tab.

    2. Result:

    • The Policy Activation tab opens.
    • The list of rules displays with an Activation Change status of New, and a Current State of Inactive.
  1. In the Policy Activation tab, click the green Activate button.
  2. Select one or more rules and select Activate from the More Actions drop-down menu.
  3. A Yes icon appears in the Activate? column for the rule(s).

To deactivate a rule:

  1. Select one or more rules and select Don’t Activate from the More Actions drop-down menu.
  1. No appears in to the Activate? column for the rule(s).

To activate all the rules:

  1. Select Activate all Rules from the More Actions drop-down menu.
  1. A Yes icon appears in the Activate? column for the rule(s).

To deactivate all rules:

  1. Select all the rules and select Don’t Activate from the More Actions drop-down menu.
  1. No appears in to the Activate? column for the rule(s).

See also Activate a Draft Policy - The Policy Activation Tab.
