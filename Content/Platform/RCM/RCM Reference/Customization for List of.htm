Customization for List of Items

The list of items customization feature enables you to customize the list display for each view.

Customization of one view does not affect the other views.

Column Width Modification

You have the ability to quickly adjust the size of columns. Place the cursor on the edge of the column heading that you want to adjust. When the display of the cursor changes to you can drag the edge in order to increase or decrease the width of the column.

A column cannot be resized to less than 30 pixels.

Columns width customization is not saved once you logout of an session.

Column Double-Click Extension

You can enable the extension of a column automatically by double-clicking on the right border of a column header.

When you double-click, the column is redefined and extended according to the relevant width for its column data. The column itself retains its wrapping definition when it is manually resized by the user.

Column Rearrangement

You have the ability to quickly move the column(s) to another portion of the list. Simply drag the column heading across the heading bar until it reaches the desired location.
