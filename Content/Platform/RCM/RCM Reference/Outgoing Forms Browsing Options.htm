Outgoing Forms Browsing Options

Whenever an Outgoing Forms List goes beyond the size of the configured row limit the list is presented in multiple pages, which groups the outgoing forms (For example: Outgoing Forms 1 – 20, 21- 30, etc.).

You can view the outgoing forms in groups of 20 or in groups of 100 outgoing forms. When the outgoing forms are presented in groups of 20, a link appears on the far right of the browsing options that lets you toggle the view to groups of 100. Once in this view, the link changes to . (If the full list is less than 100, the link toggles between Show 20 and Show all.)

Use the next and previous page options to jump to the next or previous group of items or to jump directly to the first page or the last page.
