The Settings Filter

The Filter by: feature enables you to filter the current list to show only those items that contain the entered string. To use the Filter by: feature:

  • Enter the string in the Filter by: field and click Go or press the Enter key.
  • A number on top of the field indicates the number of times that the string appears in the tree. All instances of the string are displayed and are highlighted in red.
  • Click Clear to remove the results of the filter and clear the Filter by: field.
