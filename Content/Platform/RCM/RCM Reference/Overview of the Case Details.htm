Overview of the Case Details Display

You can view full details about any case by clicking the Case ID link that is associated with each case within the main list of cases.

When case details are available, one of the fields that you see within the list is a link to the Case Details. However, since each organization has different requirements for the Case List content, a different field may be used as this link.

The Case Details information replaces the Case list on the screen.

The Case Details window includes the following subtabs:

  • General: Enables you to add/remove general information about the case.
  • Case Contents: Enables you to add/remove documents (files) to/from the case
  • Workflow: Enables you to move the case from one step to another (the steps of case are defined according to its associated case type).
  • Forms: Enables you to add/remove predefined forms such as SAR and CTR forms to the case
  • History: Enables you to view the history of actions that have been made on the case.

The available options and the case details that are presented within this window depend on the Case Type and on your organization’s requirements.
