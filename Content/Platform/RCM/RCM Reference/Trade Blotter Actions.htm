Trade Blotter Actions

The Trade Blotter actions enable you to perform various functions with the trade blotters.

The buttons that appear in the toolbar depend on the configuration required by your organization. This guide presents all available actions. If an action is not supported within your organization’s workflow, or if it is not included within your permitted views, it will not appear.

The following Trade Blotter actions are described:

Assign

Add Note

View Notes

Sign-Off

Print

Export

View History
