Overview of Work Items

The Work Items tab opens a module, which shows work items that the currently logged on user has permission to view. Work Items are information issues that are generated and displayed by the according to a solution’s specific configuration. You can filter the Work Items list according to your needs and perform various actions with listed work items.

The Work Items module enables authorized users to:

  • Review work items, assign ownership and reach decisions based on the work item’s workflow.
  • Search for work items across by free text search.
  • Filter and sort the work item’s views.
  • Add notes, attachments and email communication to the work items.
  • Print and export a list of work items or specific selected ones.
  • Create, edit and delete work items.
