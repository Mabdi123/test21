Overview of Work Items Details

The Work Item details window provides detailed information regarding the relevant work item, incident, issue or any other defined work item or entity.  You can view full details about any work item by clicking the Item ID link that is associated with each work item within the main list of work items. Since each organization has different requirements for the Work Items List content, a different field may be used as this link. The following is an example of work items details.

The Work Item Details information replaces the work items list on the screen. The layout and content of the Work Items details depends on your organization’s line of business.

The available options, work item details and layout of the Work Items Details page depend on the work item type and on your organization’s requirements.
