Work Items Views

You can select the view of work items that is appropriate for you by clicking the arrow beside the current view within the title bar of the Work Item List.

A drop-down list opens, showing all views that are appropriate for your tasks.

The views that appear within the list depend on the requirements of each organization and the roles that you perform.

The following are examples of views and options that may be available.

Option

Description

My BU Items

Shows all open work items associated with a specific business unit that the logged on user has permission to view.

All Items

Shows all open items that the logged on user has permission to view.

My Items

Shows only open items that are assigned to the user who is currently logged on to the RCM application.

Unassigned Items

Shows open work items that have not yet been assigned to any user and that the currently logged on user has permission to view.

Aging - –0 days

Enables you to view the open items that have an age of 30 days or more. Your organization has the option to define another number of days as the aging threshold. The manual item date is determined by the value in the date field when you create a new item.

Closed Items

Shows closed items that have not been archived.

Archived Items

Shows closed items that have been archived.
